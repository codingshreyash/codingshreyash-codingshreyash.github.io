About Shreyash.

My name is Shreyash Ranjan, and I am a junior at North Penn High School. I am running to serve you on the Pennsylvania FBLA Officer team as First Vice President for the academic year 2022-2023. The knowledge following the world of business hooks me, as does helping my peers develop insight further into this sector. I am people oriented and enjoy working with others.

As a part time Software Engineer at Lockheed Martin, I have the privilege of working with business analysts. Additionally, I have held leadership positions in clubs like Robotics, FBLA, Academic Decathlon, and Investment Club. The mosaic of my business exposure and my leadership experience has laid a great foundation for the role of First Vice President. My mentors find me very hardworking, responsible, and adept in the realm of communicating.

When elected, my priority will be spreading the benefits of PA FBLA to prospective students. I have stood first place in the competitive event at the State Leadership Conference and eighth at the National Leadership Conference. My experience will allow me to encourage and guide my fellow students to better benefit from FBLA. I will work with cabinet members to make PA FBLA the best state run FBLA in the nation and I will advocate in favor of FBLA to state and national legislature bodies.

I hope to see you at my booth and remember to: “RUN with RANJAN”

What I'll do as your First Vice President

These are some of the many goals I have when serving on the state officer team. Please give me your vote so I can implement these goals as actions.

Increase Membership

Due to the COVID-19 Pandemic many schools in and out of Pennsylvania were forced to not hold in-person extracurriculars, because of this student membership has been at an all time low. As 1st VP I hope to reach out to schools with inactive chapters as well as reach out to schools who do not have an FBLA chapter to allow more students to be impacted by the greatness of PA-FBLA.

Connect with Companies

If elected as 1st VP I want to connect more companies to FBLA. This will allow students to get first hand experience to see how companies are run. Connecting with companies also allows students to have a leg up to see what companies they’re interested in and perhaps pursue a career at that company.

Have more Conferences

Everyone knows one of the best things about FBLA is the conferences. As 1st VP I will advocate for more conferences throughout the year so that nearby schools can get together and hone in on skills they want to learn. These conferences will be packed with a mixture of learning and fun.

Help PA Schools

If given the opportunity to serve on the PA FBLA officer team, I will pitch to my cabinet the idea of a new state project. This state project will help raise money for schools in Pennslyvania that lack money to improve student knowledge. This money will help purchase new books and items to help students learn better. By coming together, we all can achieve this amazing goal.

Expand Student Involvement

I want to increase student involvement as much as possible. There are many great things that FBLA offers, such as the FBLA Week, but I want to ensure that students have even more events like that. These events can enhance the student experience at FBLA and guide students into being themselves.

Increase Social Media Footprint

PA FBLA has a great amount of Social Media platforms but few people know about it. I want to change that so we can reach the student body daily. This means we can share updates on local chapters, have a tighter community of PA-FBLA and share all the amazing things that the students of FBLA are up to.
