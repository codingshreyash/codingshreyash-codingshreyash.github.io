About Shreyash.

My name is Shreyash Ranjan, and I am a junior at North Penn High School. I am running to serve on the Pennsylvania FBLA Officer team as First Vice President for the academic year 2022-2023. I am interested in learning and understanding the world of business and sharing my knowledge with my fellow students. I am people oriented and enjoy working with others.

As a part time Software Engineer at Lockheed Martin, I have the privilege of working and am inspired by executives and business analysts. I hold leadership positions in clubs like Robotics, FBLA, Academic Decathlon, and Investment Club. The mosaic of my business exposure and my leadership experience has laid a great foundation for the role of First Vice President. My mentors find me very hardworking, responsible, with excellent articulation and communication skills.

When elected, my priority would be to socialize PA FBLA benefits to prospective students. I have stood first place at the State Leadership Conference and eighth at the National Leadership Conference. My experience will allow me to encourage and guide my fellow students to achieve success at FBLA. I will work with cabinet members to make PA FBLA the best state run FBLA in the nation.

I hope to see you at my booth and remember to: “RUN with RANJAN”

Goals as your First Vice President

These are some of the many goals I have when serving on the state officer team. Please give me your vote so I can implement these goals into actions.

Increase Membership

The COVID-19 Pandemic forced many schools to switch to virtual meetings for clubs like FBLA. Unfortunately, this caused membership to decline and currently is at its all-time low. As 1st VP, I will reach out to schools with inactive chapters and schools who do not have an FBLA chapter yet. This will ensure more students can benefit and be inspired by all that PA-FBLA has to offer.

Help PA Schools

If given the opportunity to serve on the PA FBLA officer team, I will pitch to my cabinet the idea of a new state project. This state project will help raise money for schools in Pennslyvania that lack money to improve student knowledge. This money will help purchase new books and items to help students learn better. By coming together, we all can achieve this amazing goal.

Increase Social Media Footprint

Even though PA FBLA is on different Social Media platforms few people know about it. I want to change that so we can reach and communicate with the students at a higher frequently. This means we can share updates on local chapters, build a tight-knit community of PA-FBLA. Be proud, share and celebrate all the amazing things that the students of FBLA are up to.

Connect with Companies

If elected as 1st VP I want to connect more companies to FBLA. This will allow students to get first hand experience to see how companies are run. Connecting with companies will allow students to discover their own interests and be inspired. This will help students get exposure to different career options.

Have more Conferences

Everyone knows one of the best things about FBLA is the conferences. As 1st VP I will advocate for more conferences throughout the year so that nearby schools can get together and hone in on skills they want to learn. These conferences will be packed with a mixture of learning and fun.

Expand Student Involvement

I want to increase student involvement as much as possible. There are many great things that FBLA offers, such as the FBLA Week, but I want to ensure that students have even more events like that. These events can enhance the student experience at FBLA and guide students into being themselves.
